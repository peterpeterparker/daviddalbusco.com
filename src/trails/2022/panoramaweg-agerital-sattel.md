Panoramaweg around the Ägerisee (or Lake Aegeri).

Moving time 2h56.

From Bus stop "Unterägeri - Center", follow the yellow signs "Panoramaweg" all the way long.

Some segments are on asphalt.

Nice view, climb not too steep and few people. It was a lovely run.

More info: https://www.aegerital-sattel.ch/erleben/wandern/Panoramaweg
