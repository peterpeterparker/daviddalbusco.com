The trail begins in Brugg and winds through fields and forests up to Hauenstein. It's a pleasant run that feels predominantly uphill, with very few people around. There are limited options for refilling water, so it's important to monitor your hydration.

Waypoints:

Brugg
Staffelegg
Hauenstein

Follow the yellow sign "5 Jura-Höhenweg".
