The trail above Savogning was truly lovely, offering breathtaking views and a sense of wildness with very few people along the way. However, there's a catch: there are no catering facilities on the route!

I brought 1.5 liters of water, but I still ended up dehydrated during the last few kilometers due to the heat wave, which explains why it took me so long to complete the trail.

So, be cautious if you decide to tackle it, but aside from that, it's an amazing and relatively easy run!

---

The trail starts and ends in Savognin.

Waypoints (really easy to follow):

- Lai Tigiel
- Motta d'Err
- Via Penza
