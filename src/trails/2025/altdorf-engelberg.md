Easy run from Altdorf to Engelberg via the Surenenpass.

Beautiful route with very few hikers or runners on the ascent. The descent is busier, especially as you get closer to Engelberg, and the train ride back can be crowded.

The climb itself is straightforward, though timing matters: last year in June I had to turn back because the pass was still covered in snow and crossing became tricky. This year, in August, the trail was completely clear.

The descent makes for a smooth, steady run since it doubles as a mountain bike track.

> Notes: this trail corresponds to Via Alpina C7: Altdorf – Engelberg
